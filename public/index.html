About

AirOn was born out of the need to understand the indoor quality effects in our daily lives and aims to bring statistical methods to model relationships between exposures and health outcomes, quantify the effect of air pollution on illnesses, and help public health officials understand the public health burden of air pollution.

We believe that clean air is a basic right and citizens need to be informed and take any recommended actions to improve their health and wellbeing.

Learn About Air Quality

Indoor Air Quality (IAQ) refers to the air quality within and around buildings and structures, especially as it relates to the health and comfort of building occupants. Understanding and controlling common pollutants indoors can help reduce your risk of indoor health concerns.
